About US

With an aim of identifying skill gaps with respect to industry standard, developing and honing these skills for our students, we have set up Computer engineering Students Association(CESA) in the year 2023-24 . We encourage all the students to get involved with CESA. Your ideas, energy, and enthusiasm are the driving force behind our organization. Whether you want to join a club, attend events, or take on a leadership role, there are countless opportunities to engage and make a difference during your time here.

Vision
  • To create a vibrant and inclusive student community where every member has the opportunity to grow, learn, and thrive.
  • To be a catalyst for positive change, fostering an environment that empowers students to become leaders, innovators, and global citizens
Mission
  • To connect, engage, and support students in their academic, personal, and professional journeys. We do this by organizing events, workshops, and initiatives that promote collaboration, personal development, and social responsibility.
  • We are committed to inclusivity, diversity, and integrity in all our activities, and we strive to be a voice for student concerns and needs.
